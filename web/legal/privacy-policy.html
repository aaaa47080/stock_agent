PI CryptoMind values your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Identity Information

  • Pi Wallet Address: Used for account identification and transaction records
  • Nickname & Profile: Display information you set on the platform

1.2 Usage Data

  • Browsing history, feature usage patterns
  • Forum posts, comments, tipping records
  • Scam reporting and governance participation
  • API request logs (IP addresses, timestamps)

1.3 Transaction Information

  • Pi payment records (PRO subscription, tips)
  • Transaction amounts, timestamps, recipient info

  • âœ… Your API keys exist only on your device

2. How We Use Data

2.1 Service Provision

  • Verify identity and maintain account security
  • Provide personalized market analysis and portfolio tracking
  • Process Pi payment transactions

2.2 Community Management

  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse
  • Execute governance penalties and appeals
  • Moderate forum content against community guidelines

2.3 Security & Protection

  • Monitor suspicious activity (abnormal logins, malicious reports)
  • Maintain system stability and data integrity

3. Data Sharing & Third Parties

3.1 Pi Network SDK

We use Pi Network official SDK for authentication and payment processing. Your wallet address and transaction info are shared with Pi Network, subject to their Privacy Policy.

3.2 OKX API

Market data is fetched via OKX public API. We do not send your personal information to OKX.

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose data when:

  • Complying with legal obligations or government requests
  • Protecting rights and safety of platform, users, or public
  • Investigating suspected illegal activities (fraud, money laundering)

4. User Rights

4.1 Access & Download

You can view and download your personal data in Settings.

4.2 Modify Data

You can update profile settings (nickname, avatar) anytime.

4.3 Account Deletion

You can request account deletion. After deletion:

  • Personal data will be removed
  • Forum posts may be retained but anonymized (shown as "Deleted User")
  • Sent Pi tips are irreversible
  • Paid membership fees are non-refundable

5. Cookies & Tracking

5.1 Necessary Cookies

We only use necessary cookies to maintain login state and language preference.

5.2 No Ad Tracking

We do not use third-party ad trackers or analytics tools.

6. Data Retention

  • Account Data: During active period + 30 days after deletion
  • Transaction Records: 7 years (financial regulation compliance)
  • Governance Records: Permanent (prevent repeat violations)
  • API Logs: 90 days

7. Children's Privacy

This platform does not offer services to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect children's personal data. Underage accounts will be deleted immediately upon discovery.

8. Cross-Border Data Transfer

Your data is primarily stored on Taiwan servers. When using Pi Network SDK, data may transfer to Pi Network's global infrastructure. We ensure all transfers comply with GDPR and international standards.

9. Data Security Measures

  • Database encryption (AES-256)
  • HTTPS encrypted transmission
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability patches
  • Restricted employee access (least privilege principle)

10. Privacy Policy Changes

We may update this policy. Major changes will be announced on the platform and require your re-confirmation.
